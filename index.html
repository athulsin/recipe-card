Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A traditional Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, and pancetta. Creamy, rich, and absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt for pasta water

Preparation

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy
  3. Whisk eggs with Parmesan and black pepper
  4. Combine hot pasta with pancetta
  5. Add egg mixture and toss quickly
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese

Fresh Caesar Salad

Crisp romaine lettuce with homemade Caesar dressing, croutons, and Parmesan cheese. A timeless classic!

Ingredients

  • 2 heads romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Preparation

  1. Wash and chop romaine lettuce
  2. Mix mayonnaise, garlic, lemon juice, and Worcestershire
  3. Toss lettuce with dressing
  4. Add croutons and Parmesan
  5. Serve immediately while crisp

Spicy Chicken Tacos

Flavorful chicken with Mexican spices, fresh vegetables, and creamy avocado. Perfect for any fiesta!

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Cilantro and lime for garnish

Preparation

  1. Season chicken with chili powder and cumin
  2. Cook chicken in a hot pan until golden
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry pan
  4. Prepare all vegetables and toppings
  5. Assemble tacos with chicken and toppings
  6. Garnish with cilantro and lime juice

Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes

Light, fluffy pancakes perfect for weekend breakfast. Serve with maple syrup and fresh berries!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • Maple syrup for serving

Preparation

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Whisk eggs, buttermilk, and melted butter
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently
  4. Heat griddle or pan over medium heat
  5. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form
  6. Flip and cook until golden brown
  7. Serve hot with maple syrup
